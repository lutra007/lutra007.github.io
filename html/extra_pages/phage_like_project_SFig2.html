SFigure 2. Genetic comparison and clustering within each phage group

Clustermaps depict the coding region similarities within each Enterobacterales family. Panel A shows the D6 family, Panel B – P1, and Panel C – SSU5. The presence of sequences similar to each reference CDS is shown. The PLPs and phages are labeled on the rows (right) and the CDSs on the columns (top).

Each CDS similarity value was calculated as: %identity*[alignment length]/[CDS length]. The resulting matrix was plotted with Python seaborn.clustermap v0.10.1 applying a Ward’s Hierarchical clustering method with Euclidean distance. The relative values are represented as a continuous red-blue colored scale palette (legend top-left).

Rows on the left are labeled with discrete colored stripes showing bacterial hosts and major replicons, and the rows on the right show GenBank IDs and ARG content.
